Sydney Kahmann

Ph.D. Candidate in Statistics

UCLA

I am a UCLA Statistics Ph.D. Candidate and NSF Graduate Research Fellow. My dissertation research is in causal inference, advised by Dr. Erin Hartman. In 2018, I co-founded the Society of Women in Statistics at UCLA, recognized and funded by UCLA and the Statistics Department.
